==Notes==
*This card's slay ability applies the '''Sanctioned''' card effect.
*This card depicts a member of the [[Lore:Morag Tong|Morag Tong]], a group of assassins devoted to [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]] and based in [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]]. Its summoning dialogue references the writs of execution employed by the Morag Tong, which allowed for the legal assassination of targets.
