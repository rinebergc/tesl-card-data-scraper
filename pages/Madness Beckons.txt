==Availability==
A playset of premium Madness Beckons cards was available during the [[Legends:Festival of Madness|Festival of Madness]] on day 39 and 40 for 2,000 gold as part of the second Isle of Madness Sneak Peek Pack. Before the release of Isle of Madness, it couldn't be soul-summoned, instead showing the message "Available only at the whim of Sheogorath".

==Notes==
*This card depicts [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]] sitting on the Throne of Madness, as they appeared in the ''[[Shivering:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]]'' expansion for ''[[OB:Oblivion|TES IV: Oblivion]]''.
