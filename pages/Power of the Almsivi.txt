==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card applies the '''Wrath of the Three''' card effect.
*The card depicts and is named for the [[Lore:Tribunal|Tribunal]] (or Almsivi), [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'s deities from the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]] to the end of the [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]].
