==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck as a potential reward from [[LG:Bandit Ambush (quest)|Bandit Ambush]].
