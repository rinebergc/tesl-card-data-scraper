==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Docks|Docks]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Docks, as well as being offered as a Top 1000 reward in the 9/1/2024 Grand Melee.
