==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card is named for the [[Skyrim:Ice Storm|Ice Storm]] spell, which first appeared in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''.
*The card formerly used the art that is now found on {{Card|Winter's Grasp}}.
*The card's art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
