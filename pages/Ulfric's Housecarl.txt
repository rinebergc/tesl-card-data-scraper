==Availability==
Ulfric's Housecarl was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for June 2017. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*The card's health was formerly 4, until this was decreased in [[Legends:Patch/1.69.0|Patch 1.69.0]].
*The card depicts [[Skyrim:Galmar Stone-Fist|Galmar Stone-Fist]], who appeared in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'' as the housecarl of [[Skyrim:Ulfric Stormcloak|Ulfric Stormcloak]].
*The card's summon audio is an excerpt from Galmar's dialogue when asked about the aim of the war after you join the Stormcloaks:
::''"First we'll kick the Thalmor and their bloody Imperial puppets out of the country. Then we'll rebuild Skyrim into the land she once was."''
*The card's attack audio is an excerpt from a conversation during the quest [[Skyrim:Joining the Stormcloaks|Joining the Stormcloaks]]:
::'''Galmar''': ''"The people are behind you."''
::'''Ulfric''': ''"Many I fear still need convincing."''
::'''Galmar''': ''"Then let them die with their false kings."''
