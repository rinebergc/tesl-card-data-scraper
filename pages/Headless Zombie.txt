==Availability==
Headless Zombie was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for January 2020. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards after February 2019, the premium version was awarded to the top 1000 [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]] players or can be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*This card depicts the [[OB:Headless Zombie|Headless Zombie]] enemy type as they appeared in [[OB:Oblivion|''TES IV: Oblivion'']].
