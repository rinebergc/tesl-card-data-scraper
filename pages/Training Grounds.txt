==Availability==
Training Grounds was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for February 2019. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. The premium version was awarded to the top 1,000 Legend ranks, and otherwise can be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*The card's ability applies the '''Training's Over''' card effect.
