==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Paarthurnax's Roar|Paarthurnax's Roar]] theme deck.
==Notes==
*This card's summon ability applies the '''Murkwater Curse''' card effect.
*The card is named for the [[Online:Goblin#Murkwater Tribe|Murkwater Tribe]] of [[Lore:Goblin|Goblin]]s, who appeared in ''[[Online:Online|ESO]]'s'' group dungeon [[Online:Fungal Grotto|Fungal Grotto]], which the card's art also depicts.
