==Availability==
Worm King's Agent was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for November 2017. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.

== Notes ==
*The name of the card references [[Lore:Mannimarco|Mannimarco]], an ancient lich referred to as the King of Worms. His fanatical cult of [[Lore:Necromancy|necromancers]] called themselves the [[Lore:Order of the Black Worm|Order of the Black Worm]].
