==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck.

==Notes== 
*The card art depicts [[Morrowind:Nileno Dorvayn|Nileno Dorvayn]], [[Morrowind:House Hlaalu|House Hlaalu]]'s steward in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'', holding a Redoran Bonemold Helm, a reference to the House Hlaalu quest [[Morrowind:Disguise|Disguise]].
