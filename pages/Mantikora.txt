==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable as a reward from [[Legends:The Siege of the Imperial City|The Siege of the Imperial City]], added automatically to the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] and the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck.

==Notes==
*The card originally had '''[[Legends:Guard|Guard]]''', and its '''Summon''' ability was ''"Destroy an enemy creature."''
**The card's '''Summon''' ability was changed in [[Legends:Patch/1.59.3|Patch 1.59.3]].
**'''Guard''' was removed from the card in [[Legends:Patch/1.71.2|Patch 1.71.2]]. It was added back in [[Legends:Patch/2.15|Patch 2.15]].
*[[Online:Mantikora|Mantikora]]s first appeared in [[Online:Online|ESO]].
