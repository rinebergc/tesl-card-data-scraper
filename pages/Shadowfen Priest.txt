==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Aela's Companions|Aela's Companions]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck as a reward from [[LG:The Restless Dead|The Restless Dead]].
