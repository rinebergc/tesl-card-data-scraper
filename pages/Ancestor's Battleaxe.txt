==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Heroes of Skyrim|Heroes of Skyrim]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Aela's Companions|Aela's Companions]] theme deck.

* This card's summon ability applies the '''Elder Energies''' card effect.
