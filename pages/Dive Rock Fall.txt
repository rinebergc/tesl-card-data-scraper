==Notes==
*This card depicts [[OB:Dive Rock|Dive Rock]] from ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion]]''. It is a reference to a popular player method of executing the [[OB:Adoring Fan|Adoring Fan]] follower by dismissing him at the very edge of Dive Rock, causing him to turn and instantly plunge to his death.
