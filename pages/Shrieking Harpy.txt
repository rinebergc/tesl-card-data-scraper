==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck as a reward from [[LG:The Brawl|The Brawl]].

==Notes==
*This card originally was of common rarity, but was changed around 10/2016 to help balance quality of common cards. {{ref|name=LetterDevs|[https://legends.bethesda.net/articles/letter-from-the-devs Letter from the Devs] on Bethesda.net, September 29, 2016}}
