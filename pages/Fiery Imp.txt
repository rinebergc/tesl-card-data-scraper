==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Aela's Companions|Aela's Companions]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck.

==Notes==
* This card originally was of common rarity, but was changed around October 2016 to help balance quality of common cards. {{ref|name=LetterDevs|[https://legends.bethesda.net/articles/letter-from-the-devs Letter from the Devs] on Bethesda.net, September 29, 2016}}
*[[Lore:Imp|Imp]]s first appeared in ''[[Daggerfall:Imp|Daggerfall]]'', and later reappeared in ''[[Oblivion:Imp|Oblivion]]'' and [[Online:Imp|ESO]].
