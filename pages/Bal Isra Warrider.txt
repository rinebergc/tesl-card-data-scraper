==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck.
==Notes==
*This card is named for {{Lore Link|Bal Isra}}, an area in the western [[Lore:Ashlands|Ashlands]] of [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]] that previously [[Morrowind:Bal Isra|appeared]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|TES III: Morrowind]]''.
