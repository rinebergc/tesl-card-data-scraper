His ability is, in essence, an unsummon and resummon effect, thus removing any buffs and/or damage, and triggering any Summon abilities. It could also be used to temporarily remove a [[Legends:Guard|Guard]] creature to give your creatures a chance to attack your opponent.

==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Heroes of Skyrim|Heroes of Skyrim]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck.
