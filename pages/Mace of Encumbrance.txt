==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck.
==Notes==
*Generic glass maces called [[OB:Mace of Encumbrance|Mace of Encumbrance]] were featured in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]'' as random loot, though the depiction in the card's art is more akin to ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'''s [[SR:Glass Mace|Glass Mace]]s.
*The knights in the card's art are [[Lore:Breton|Breton]]s clad in [[Online:Breton Style|Breton armor]] as it appeared in [[ON:Online|ESO]].
*A mace of encumbrance appears in the hand of the alternate-art [[Legends:Master of Arms|Master of Arms]].
