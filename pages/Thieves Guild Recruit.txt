==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], three copies are included in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Brynjolf's Heist|Brynjolf's Heist]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Paarthurnax's Roar|Paarthurnax's Roar]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card was previously part of the [[Legends:Training|training]] mechanic where it could be upgraded from {{Card|Guild Recruit}} at level 6.
*The card's summon ability applies the '''Got the Loot''' card effect.
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]], which first [[Daggerfall:Thieves Guild|appeared]] in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]'', and later reappeared in [[Morrowind:Thieves Guild|''Morrowind'']], [[Oblivion:Thieves Guild|''Oblivion'']], [[Skyrim:Thieves Guild (faction)|''Skyrim'']], and [[Online:Thieves Guild|ESO]].
*The card's summon ability was originally ''"Draw a card. If it costs 7 or more, reduce its cost by 2."'', until this was changed in [[Legends:Patch/1.69.0|Patch 1.69.0]].
