==Notes==
*The {{Card|Recruit|Recruits}} created by this card are random for each lane.
*This card applies the '''Battle Ready''' card effect.
*This card is a reference to the book [[Lore:Those Who Stood at Chalman Keep|''Those Who Stood at Chalman Keep'']], which describes a [[Online:Abbreviations and Terms#PvP|PvP]] battle that took place at [[ON:Chalman Keep|Chalman Keep]] during the [[ON:Beta|beta]] for [[ON:Online|ESO]]. The card art depicts a group of [[Legends:Daggerfall Covenant|Daggerfall Covenant]] soldiers holding the keep against two enemy armies from the opposing alliances. For more information, see the [[Lore:Fort Chalman|lore article]].
