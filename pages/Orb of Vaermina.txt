==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is obtainable as a potential reward from [[Legends:Interlude|Interlude]]. If rewarded, it will be added to the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]], [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]], [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]], and [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] decks.

==Notes==
*The stolen card is a copy and will not be removed from the opponent's deck.
*The [[Lore:Orb of Vaermina|Orb]], an object associated with [[Lore:Vaermina|Vaermina]], previously appeared during a [[Oblivion:Vaermina|quest]] in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''.
*The card's art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
