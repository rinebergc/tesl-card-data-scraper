==Availability==
Murkwater Scourge was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for March 2019. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards after February 2019, the premium version was awarded to the top 1000 [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]] players or can be obtained through soul-summoning.
