==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:The Caminus Pit|The Caminus Pit]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of The Caminus Pit.

==Notes==
*This card's summon ability applies the '''Filthy Skeever''' card effect.
*The card's subtype was originally Skeever, until Automaton was added in September 2018.
