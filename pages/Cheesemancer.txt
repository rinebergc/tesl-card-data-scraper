Each Cheesemancer has the chance to randomly gain one of the following abilities at the start of the game:
*'''[[Legends:Pilfer|Pilfer]]:''' Reduce the cost of the top card of your deck by 3. ({{Card|Cyriel}}'s ability)
*'''[[Legends:Summon|Summon]]:''' Draw an item of your choice from your deck. ({{Card|Crucible Blacksmith}}'s ability)
*'''[[Legends:Summon|Summon]]:''' '''[[Legends:Battle|Battle]]''' an enemy creature.<br>When Cheesemancer takes damage, she deals that much damage to you. ({{Card|Trouble Seeker}}'s ability)
*Immune to '''[[Legends:Lethal|Lethal]]'''.<br>Immune to '''[[Legends:Shackle|Shackle]]'''.<br>Your opponent can't target Cheesemancer with actions. ({{Card|Hulking Scalon}}'s ability)
*Only one creature in this lane can attack each turn. ({{Card|The Gatekeeper}}'s ability)

==Availability==
A playset of premium Cheesemancer cards was available during the [[Legends:Festival of Madness|Festival of Madness]] on day 29 and 30 for 2,000 gold as part of the Isle of Madness Sneak Peek Pack. Before the release of Isle of Madness, it couldn't be soul-summoned, instead showing the message "Available only at the whim of Sheogorath".
