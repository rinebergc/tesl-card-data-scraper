==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*[[Morrowind:Golden Saint|Golden Saint]]s first appeared in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'', and later reappeared in ''[[Shivering:Golden Saint|Shivering Isles]]'' and [[Online:Golden Saint|ESO]]. For more information on Golden Saints, see the [[Lore:Golden Saint|lore article]].
*Golden Saints are also featured in the cards {{Card|Brilliant Experiment}}, {{Card|Golden Initiate}}, and {{Card|Shining Saint}}
*The card text was changed to remove "Copy" from the textbox in [[Legends:Patch/2.12|Patch 2.12]].
