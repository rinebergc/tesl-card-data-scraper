==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck.

==Notes==
*This card was previously part of the [[Legends:Training|training]] mechanic where it could be upgraded from {{Card|Valenwood Trapper}} at level 2.
*This was originally a rare card, but was changed around 10/2016 to help balance quality of common cards. {{ref|name=LetterDevs|[https://legends.bethesda.net/articles/letter-from-the-devs Letter from the Devs] on Bethesda.net, September 29, 2016}}
*The card is named for the nation of [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]].
*Playing the card's summon ability counts towards the '''Arrows Fired''' [[Legends:Statistics|statistic]].
