==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], three copies are included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Brynjolf's Heist|Brynjolf's Heist]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck as a reward from [[LG:The Brawl|The Brawl]].

==Notes==
*An alternate-art version of this card was given out as a participation reward for the December 2017 [[Legends:Grand Melee|Grand Melee]].
