A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Accuse Varlyn|Accuse Varlyn]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Accuse Varlyn.
