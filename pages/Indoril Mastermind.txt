==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, two copies are included in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.
