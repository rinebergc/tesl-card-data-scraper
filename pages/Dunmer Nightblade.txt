==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck.

==Notes==
*Nightblade is a class that has appeared in many ''Elder Scrolls'' games.
*The card's summon audio, ''"By Dawn and Dusk, I move as a shadow"'', refers to the [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Prince]] [[Lore:Azura|Azura]].
* This card originally gave a {{Card|Steel Sword}}, but this was changed to {{Card|Iron Sword}}  November 2016 because it was too "[[Legends:Terminology#Reach|bursty]]".{{ref|name=LetterDevs|[[Legends:Patch/1.59.7.2610|Letter from the Devs]] on Bethesda.net, November 16, 2016}}
