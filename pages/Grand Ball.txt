==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Arenthil Wethrin's House|Arenthil Wethrin's House]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Arenthil Wethrin's House.

==Notes==
*Though the card art uses [[Skyrim:Dragonsreach|Dragonsreach]] as a reference point for the background, it is intended to depict a [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] gala in [[Lore:Wayrest|Wayrest]].
