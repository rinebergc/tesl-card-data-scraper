==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:The Halls of Regulation|The Halls of Regulation]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of The Halls of Regulation.

==Notes==
*The card was originally called '''Factotum Exterminator''' and had two extra summon effects: gaining 2 health and dealing 2 damage to the opponent. It also originally used the art that was later used for {{Card|Assembled Sentry}}.{{Ref|name=gstaff|1=[https://www.reddit.com/r/elderscrollslegends/comments/79vy8r/the_next_story_expansion_return_to_clockwork_city/dp5arjf/?context=3 The Next Story Expansion: Return to Clockwork City - Gstaff, Reddit Oct 31, 2017]}}
*Assembled Sanitizers first appeared as [[ON:Factotum Sanitizer|Factotum Sanitizer]]s in [[ON:Online|ESO]] with the ''[[ON:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]]'' update.
