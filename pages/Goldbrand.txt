==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is obtainable as a reward from [[Legends:Reive's Wrath|Reive's Wrath]]. It will be added to the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]], [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]], [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]], and [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] decks.

==Notes==
*[[Morrowind:Goldbrand|Goldbrand]] first appeared in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'', and later [[Oblivion:Goldbrand|reappeared]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''. For historical information, see the [[Lore:Goldbrand|lore article]].
*The [[Legends:Titles|title]] '''The Golden''' is achieved by dealing 8 damage in one activation of this card; currently the only ways to do so is to use Goldbrand in conjunction with either {{Card|Tower Alchemist}} or {{Card|Cauldron Keeper}}.
