The following cards will have their cost reduced:
*[[Legends:Created Cards|Created]] Cards
*[[Legends:Double Cards|Double]] Cards
*[[Legends:Steal|Stolen]] Cards
*[[Legends:Transform|Transformed]] Cards

[[Legends:Change|Changed]] cards and cards with [[Legends:Beast Form|Beast Form]] will not have their cost reduced, as they are still considered the same card.

==Availability==
Skooma Cat's Whimsy was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for August 2020. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards after February 2019, the premium version was awarded to the top 1000 [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]] players or can be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*This card depicts the Skooma Cat, [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath's]] [[Lore:Alfiq|Alfiq]] form, as he [[ON:Skooma Cat|appeared]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]].
