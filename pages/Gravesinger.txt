==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Cultists' Meeting Place|Cultists' Meeting Place]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Cultists' Meeting Place.

==Notes==
*When there are no cards (including actions, items, and supports) in one's discard pile, this card will produce a [[Legends:Sweet Roll|Sweet Roll]].
*This card's ability applies the '''Second Burial''' card effect.
*Gravesinger is a term for a [[Lore:Reachman|Reachman]] necromancer.
*The alternate art is based on the necromancer's [[Online:Flame Skull|Flame Skull]] skill from [[ON:Online|ESO]].
