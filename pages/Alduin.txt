==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Heroes of Skyrim|Heroes of Skyrim]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*Dragons summoned by Alduin gain the '''Child of Akatosh''' effect.
*An alternate-art version of Alduin was available in the 2017 [[Legends:New Life Festival Pack|New Life Festival Pack]].
*[[Skyrim:Alduin|Alduin]] first appeared in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|''Skyrim'']]. For more information about Alduin, see the [[Lore:Alduin|lore article]].
*The card's art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
*The card's original art depicted Alduin with four legs, but was changed in [[Legends:Patch/1.66.0|Patch 1.66]]. 
*Alduin's entrance line, "Ziil gro dovah ulse", means "Ever-bound dragon spirit", and in ''Skyrim'' it was how he addressed the dragon Sahloknir when resurrecting him.
*Alduin's attack line, "Daar lein los dii!", means "This world is mine!", and was one of his taunts in ''Skyrim''.
