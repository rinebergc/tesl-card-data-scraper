In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck, and three copies in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck.
