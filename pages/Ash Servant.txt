==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck.

==Notes==
*An alternate-artwork version of the card is available in the [[Legends:Starter Pack|Starter Pack]].
*The card's alternate art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
*The card's art appears to depict an [[Lore:Ash Spawn|Ash Spawn]], which first appeared in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''{{'}}s ''[[Skyrim:Dragonborn|Dragonborn]]'' add-on.
