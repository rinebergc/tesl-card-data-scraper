==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], three copies are included in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck.

== Notes ==
*The card's image depicts a scene from [[ON:Online|ESO]]: the [[ON:Piercing Javelin|ability of the same name]], wielded by an [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]] clad in the [[Online:High Elf Style|armor of his race]], while its alternate art depicts a [[Lore:Winged Twilight|Winged Twilight]] being destroyed by the same ability.
*The card was renamed from [[Legends:Burning Light|Burning Light]] (also a [[Online:Burning Light|skill]] in ESO) during the open beta period.
*Alternate-art versions of the card were given as a reward for entry in the July 2017 and March 2018 [[Legends:Gauntlet#Grand Melee|Grand Melee]]s.
