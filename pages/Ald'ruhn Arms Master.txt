==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, two copies are included in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card is named for [[Lore:Ald'ruhn|Ald'ruhn]], a town on [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]] which first [[Morrowind:Ald'ruhn|appeared]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'', and later [[Online:Ald'ruhn|reappeared]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]].
*The card's art may be intended to depict [[Morrowind:Vonden Mano|Vonden Mano]], A Redoran Master of Arms who is stationed in Practice Room in the Redoran Council Hall in Under-Skar. 
*Alternately, it may depict an [[Lore:Ashlander|Ashlander]] wearing their garb as it [[Online:Ashlander Style|appeared]] in ESO.
