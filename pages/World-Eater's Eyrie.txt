==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Strength of Sovngarde''' card effect.
*This card is a reference to [[Lore:Skuldafn|Skuldafn]], which was the location of the quest [[Skyrim:The World-Eater's Eyrie|The World-Eater's Eyrie]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''.
**The card art depicts a person riding on the back of [[Lore:Odahviing|Odahviing]], which was how the player was transported to Skuldafn in ''Skyrim''.
