==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*Despite the creature type, Fetcherfly Golems are not [[Lore:Elemental Daedra|elemental daedra]], but are instead just simple [[Lore:Golem|golems]].
*Fetcherfly Golems first [[ON:Hive Golem|appeared]] in ''[[Online:Online|Elder Scrolls Online: Morrowind]]''.
