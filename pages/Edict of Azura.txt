==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.

== Notes ==
*This card's name and artwork were changed from "''Raze''" at some point prior to the open beta period. Raze's artwork would later be used for {{Card|Burn and Pillage}}.
*The card is named for [[Lore:Azura|Azura]], one of the 16 [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Princes]].
