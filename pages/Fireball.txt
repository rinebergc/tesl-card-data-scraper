==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck.
==Notes==
*Destroying creatures with this card counts towards the '''Creatures Burned''' [[Legends:Statistics|statistic]].
*In pre-release materials, this card was shown with the artwork later used for {{Card|Firebolt}}.
*The card's art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
*The card's art depicts, from front, a figure in [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial Legion]] armor as it [[Skyrim:Imperial Light Armor|appeared]] in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|''Skyrim'']], a figure in the armor of [[Lore:Whiterun|Whiterun]], again as it [[Skyrim:Whiterun  Guard's Armor|appeared]] in ''Skyrim'', and a figure in ''Skyrim'''s [[Skyrim:Iron Armor|Iron Armor]].
