==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Cultists' Meeting Place|Cultists' Meeting Place]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Cultists' Meeting Place.
