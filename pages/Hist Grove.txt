==Availability==
Hist Grove was the [[LG:Monthly Cards|monthly card]] for the month of September 2016, during the open beta period. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning. Additionally, following the month that Hist Grove was awarded, it was also given out as an [[Legends:Arena|Arena]] reward.

==Notes==
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Hist|Hist]], the mystical trees of [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]].
*The card originally cost 3 and had the ongoing ability ''"When you have 15 or more max magicka, sacrifice this to summon an 8/8 '''{{Card|Swamp Leviathan}}''' in each lane."''
**The card's ongoing ability was changed in [[Legends:Patch/1.60.1|Patch 1.60.1]].
**The card's cost was increased in [[Legends:Patch/1.69.0|Patch 1.69.0]].
