==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card depicts [[Lore:Conoon Chodala|Conoon Chodala]], who appeared in ''[[Morrowind:Conoon Chodala|Morrowind]]'' and [[Online:Chodala|ESO]] as a false incarnate of the [[Lore:Nerevarine|Nerevarine]]. He is holding [[Lore:Sunna'rah|Sunna'rah]], a key [[ON:Sunna'rah|item]] from the main quest of [[Online:Morrowind|ESO: ''Morrowind'']].
