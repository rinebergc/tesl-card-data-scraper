==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card applies the '''Marked for Death''' card effect.
*The name of the card references the writs of execution employed by the [[Lore:Morag Tong|Morag Tong]], a group of assassins devoted to [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]] and based in [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]], which allowed for the legal assassination of targets. The assassin is garbed in the armor of his order as it [[Skyrim:Morag Tong Armor|appeared]] in the ''[[Skyrim:Dragonborn|Dragonborn]]'' expansion for ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'', while his target wears the [[SR:Imperial Armor|armor]] employed by the [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial Legion]] during the [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]].
