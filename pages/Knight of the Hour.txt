==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Order of the Hour Barracks|Order of the Hour Barracks]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Order of the Hour Barracks.
