==Availability==
Monster Perfection Lab was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for July 2020. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards after February 2019, the premium version was awarded to the top 1000 [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]] players or can be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*This is the only Dual-Attribute Support card.
