==Availability==
Conjuration Tutor was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for June 2017. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards before February 2019, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.
