==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*[[Morrowind:Ash Vampire|Ash Vampire]]s first appeared in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''.
*The card previously had 5 health, but this was decreased in [[Legends:Patch/1.72.4|patch 1.72.4]].
*This likely depicts [[Morrowind:Dagoth Uthol|Dagoth Uthol]], who appears in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''. This card depicts the same environment he appears in and also has his dialogue as his voice.
