==Availability==
Frenzied Alit was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for November 2018. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Frenzy''' effect to itself.
