==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, two copies are included in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The magical walls on the art of the card read "doubt is the key to certainty bars the koor" in [[Lore:Daedric_Alphabet|Daedric letters]]. The last word probably means "door".
