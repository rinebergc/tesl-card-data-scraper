==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck.

==Notes==
*Creatures that died before Disciple of Namira entered a lane do not cause the effect to trigger.
*The card is named for [[Lore:Namira|Namira]], one of the [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Princes]].
*This card was nerfed by changing its text from an instantaneous effect to an "at end of each turn" effect in [[Legends:Patch/2.11|Patch 2.11]].
