==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card's ability applies the '''Haunting''' card effect.
*The card's magicka cost was increased from 3 to 4 in [[Legends:Patch/1.72.4|patch 1.72.4]].
*The card depicts the [[Morrowind:St. Olms Haunted Manor|St. Olms Haunted Manor]], which first appeared in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''.
