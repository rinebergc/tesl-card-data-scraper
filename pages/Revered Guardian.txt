==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Heroes of Skyrim|Heroes of Skyrim]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck.
==Notes==
*The card art depicts a [[Skyrim:Dragon#Revered_DragonDG|Revered Dragon]], which first appeared in the ''[[Skyrim:Dawnguard|Dawnguard]]'' DLC for ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''.
*The artwork of this card was created by [https://www.artstation.com/artwork/xD3dnX Yintion J - Jiang Geping]. According to him, it depicts [[Skyrim:Naaslaarum|Naaslaarum]].
