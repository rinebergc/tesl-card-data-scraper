==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], three copies are included in the [[Legends:Aela's Companions|Aela's Companions]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.
