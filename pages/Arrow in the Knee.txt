==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck.

==Notes==
* This card's name is a reference to a line of [[Skyrim:Guard Dialogue|guard dialogue]] in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]: "''I used to be an adventurer like you. Then I took an arrow in the knee...''"
* The card was originally named "Arrow to the Knee", as seen in one of the first screenshots on Bethesda.net.
