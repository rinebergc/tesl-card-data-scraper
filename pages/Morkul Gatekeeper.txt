==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], three copies are included in the [[Legends:Aela's Companions|Aela's Companions]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card's summon ability applies the '''Lower the Gates''' card effect.
*An alternate-art version of the card was awarded to participants in the May 2018 [[Legends:Grand Melee|Grand Melee]].
*The card originally did not have '''Prophecy''', but it was added in 11/2016 because Orcs were weaker compared to other races. {{ref|name=LetterDevs|[[Legends:Patch/1.59.7.2610|Letter from the Devs]] on Bethesda.net, November 16, 2016}}
*The card is named for the [[Online:Morkul Stronghold|Morkul Stronghold]], which appeared in [[Online:Online|ESO]].
*The card is named "Orc Gatekeeper" in the audio files.
