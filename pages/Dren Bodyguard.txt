==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.
