==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Silverhome on the Water, Uther's Suite|Silverhome on the Water]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Silverhome on the Water.

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Raider's Inspiration''' card effect.
*The card is an homage to ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'', in which [[Lore:Penitus Oculatus|Penitus Oculatus]] agents [[Skyrim:Death Incarnate|raided]] a Dark Brotherhood sanctuary.
