==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck.
