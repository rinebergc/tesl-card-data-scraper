==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck.

*The name of the card references the [[Lore:Order of the Black Worm|Order of the Black Worm]], a fanatical cult of [[Lore:Necromancy|necromancers]] devoted to [[Lore:Mannimarco|Mannimarco]], an ancient lich referred to as the King of Worms.
