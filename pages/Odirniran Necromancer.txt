==Notes==
*This card is named after the Velothi tower Odirniran, which first [[Morrowind:Odirniran (place)|appeared]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'' and later appeared in [[Online:Online|ESO]] as [[Online:Barilzar's Tower|Barilzar's Tower]]. The card likely depicts [[Morrowind:Milyn Faram|Milyn Faram]], a [[Lore:House Telvanni|House Telvanni]] necromancer who resided there in ''Morrowind''.
