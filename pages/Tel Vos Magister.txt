==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, one copy is included in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card's ongoing ability was previously ''"At the end of your turn, you gain a Ward"'', until this was changed in [[Legends:Patch/1.72.4|patch 1.72.4]].
*The card is named for [[Lore:Tel Vos|Tel Vos]], which first [[Morrowind:Tel Vos|appeared]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'', and later [[Online:Tel Vos|reappeared]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]].
*The card's art depicts [[Morrowind:Aryon|Aryon]], who first appeared in ''Morrowind'' as Tel Vos's magister.
