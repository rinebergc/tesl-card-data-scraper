==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck as a potential reward from [[LG:Bandit Ambush (quest)|Bandit Ambush]].

*A card named ''"[[:File:LG-card-Shimmering Scimitar.png|Shimmering Scimitar]]"'' previously used this card's artwork, but the card became ''"{{Card|Sentinel Battlemace}}"'' and was given new art.
