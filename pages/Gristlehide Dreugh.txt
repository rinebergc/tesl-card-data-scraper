==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Dreughside|Dreughside]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Dreughside.

==Notes==
*[[Lore:Dreugh|Dreugh]] are a race of crab-people who have appeared in ''[[Daggerfall:Dreugh|Daggerfall]]'', ''[[Morrowind:Dreugh|Morrowind]]'', ''[[Oblivion:Land Dreugh|Oblivion]]'', and [[Online:Dreugh|ESO]]. They have been associated with [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]], and are reputed to have ruled over the oceans of the world long ago.
