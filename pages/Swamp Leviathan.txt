==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck as a reward from [[LG:Broadsides|Broadsides]] ''[the initial deck has two copies, you gain a third from this battle]''.
