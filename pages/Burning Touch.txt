==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card art depicts a [[Skyrim:Frost Troll|frost troll]] (as seen in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'') being burned by magical fire. In earlier games, trolls were notoriously difficult to kill due to their regenerative abilities but were susceptible to fire damage.
