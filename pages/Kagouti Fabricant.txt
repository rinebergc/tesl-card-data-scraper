==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:The Betrayal|The Betrayal]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of The Betrayal.

==Notes==
*Kagouti Fabricants first [[Online:Kagouti Fabricant|appeared]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]], with the ''[[ON:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]]'' update.
*The card text was changed to remove "Copy" from the textbox in [[Legends:Patch/2.12|Patch 2.12]].
