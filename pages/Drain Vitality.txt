==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Heroes of Skyrim|Heroes of Skyrim]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Paarthurnax's Roar|Paarthurnax's Roar]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card's ability applies the '''Vitality Drained''' card effect.
*The card previously gave all enemy creatures in play -2/-2, but was nerfed in [[Legends:Patch/1.72.4|patch 1.72.4]].
*The card is based on [[Skyrim:Drain Vitality|Drain Vitality]], a shout which first appeared in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''{{'}}s ''[[Skyrim:Dawnguard|Dawnguard]]'' add-on.
*The [[Legends:Grand Melee|Grand Melee]] than ran from January 28-31, 2022 rewarded up to three [[Legends:Premium|premium]] alternate art Drain Vitality cards, one for each run entered.
