==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, two copies are included in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Orvas Takes''' card effect to the enemy creature and '''Orvas Gives''' to the friendly creature..
*This card depicts [[Morrowind:Orvas Dren|Orvas Dren]] who appeared in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''.
