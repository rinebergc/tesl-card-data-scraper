==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Jaws of Oblivion|Jaws of Oblivion]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Mankar's Paradise|Mankar's Paradise]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card depicts a [[Lore:Scamp|scamp]], a type of [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]] primarily associated with [[Lore:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]]. An Oblivion Gate, a portal to the [[Lore:Deadlands|Deadlands]], can be seen in the background.
*The art for this card was created by freelance artist Pedro Otelcana.
