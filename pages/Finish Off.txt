==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Dagoth's Might|Dagoth's Might]] theme deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Paarthurnax's Roar|Paarthurnax's Roar]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck as a reward from [[LG:Unwelcome Intrusion|Unwelcome Intrusion]].
