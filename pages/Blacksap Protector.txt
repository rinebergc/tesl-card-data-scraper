==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*An alternate-art version of this card is available in the [[LG:Starter Pack|Starter Pack]].
*The card originally was of common rarity, but was changed around 10/2016 to help balance quality of common cards. {{ref|name=LetterDevs|[https://legends.bethesda.net/articles/letter-from-the-devs Letter from the Devs] on Bethesda.net, September 29, 2016}}
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Blacksap Rebellion|Blacksap Rebellion]].
*The card's alternate-art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
