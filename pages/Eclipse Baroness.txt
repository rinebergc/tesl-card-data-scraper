==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Eclipse Hideout|Eclipse Hideout]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Eclipse Hideout.  An alternate art version was offered as part of the 2019 [[Legends:Festival of Madness|Festival of Madness]].
