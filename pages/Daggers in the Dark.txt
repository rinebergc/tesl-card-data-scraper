==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for examining the [[Legends:Murder Scene|Murder Scene]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the murder scene has been examined.

==Notes==
*The card's art depicts two [[Lore:Dunmer|Dark Elves]] on the docks of [[Lore:Wayrest|Wayrest]].
*The card's art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
