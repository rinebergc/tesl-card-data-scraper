==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck.
==Notes==
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]] city of [[Lore:Sentinel|Sentinel]].
*The card was originally named "Shimmering Scimitar" in prerelease materials, but by the time of open beta it was renamed "Sentinel Battlemace" and had Breakthrough dropped, and the original art was used for {{Card|Steel Scimitar}}.
*This is the only Dual-Attribute Item card.
