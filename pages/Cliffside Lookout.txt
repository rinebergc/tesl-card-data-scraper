==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Heroes of Skyrim|Heroes of Skyrim]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Forewarned''' card effect.
*The card's attacking audio, ''"For Falkreath!"'', refers to the [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]] town of [[Lore:Falkreath|Falkreath]], which first [[Arena:Falcrenth|appeared]] in [[Arena:Arena|''Arena'']], and later [[Skyrim:Falkreath|reappeared]] in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|''Skyrim'']] and in [[Online:Falkreath Hold|ESO]].
