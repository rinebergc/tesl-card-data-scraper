==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:The Red Mountain|The Red Mountain]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of The Red Mountain.

==Notes==
*This creature's on-summon quote comes from the song "[[Skyrim:Bardic Performances#The Dragonborn Comes|The Dragonborn Comes]]", which was sung by bards in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''.
*This card depicts [[Skyrim:Lynly Star-Sung|Lynly Star-Sung]] from ''Skyrim''. She is depicted with black hair as described by [[SR:Sibbi Black-Briar|Sibbi Black-Briar]], and not as she physically appears in ''Skyrim'' with blonde hair.
