==Notes==
*The card's art likely depicts the [[Lore:Riften|Riften]] marketplace. Some objects of note are [[Lore:Azura's Star|Azura's Star]], the [[Lore:Dragon Priest Masks|mask]] of a [[Lore:Dragon Priest|Dragon Priest]], a bowl of [[Lore:Briar Heart|briar hearts]], a bucket of [[Skyrim:Other Unique Items|paragons]], the [[SR:Queen Bee Statue|Queen Bee Statue]] that could be [[SR:The Litany of Larceny|pilfered]] in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'', and a [[Lore:Skooma|skooma]] bottle in the adventurer's hand. Additionally, a [[Lore:Stalhrim|stalhrim]] sword and a pair of [[Lore:Malachite|glass]] daggers are present in the full art, but cropped on the card itself.
