==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is obtainable in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck as a potential reward from [[LG:Unwelcome Intrusion|Unwelcome Intrusion]]. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck as a potential Reward from [[LG:Unwelcome Intrusion|Unwelcome Intrusion]].

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Nature's Wrath''' card effect.
*[[Daggerfall:Spriggan|Spriggan]]s first appeared in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]'', and have since appeared in every mainline game in the series.
