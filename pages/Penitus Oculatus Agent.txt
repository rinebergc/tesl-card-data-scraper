==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Bravil Treasury|Bravil Treasury]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Bravil Treasury.
