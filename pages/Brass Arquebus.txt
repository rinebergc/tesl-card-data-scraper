==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:The Last Stand|The Last Stand]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of The Last Stand.

==Notes==
*This card is named "Refabricated Arquebus" in the game-files, which would have referred to a Dwarven Sphere from [[Online:Refabricated Arquebus|ESO]].
