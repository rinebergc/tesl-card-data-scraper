==Notes==
*This card depicts a [[Battlespire:Seducer|Seducer]] in [[Battlespire:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]]'s realm, as was encountered in ''[[Battlespire:Battlespire|Battlespire]]''. Her skin tone matches Seducers found in that game, however, the realm resembles its [[Oblivion:Planes of Oblivion|appearance]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]'' rather than ''Battlespire'', and the armor worn by the Seducer resembles the [[Shivering:Dark Seducer|Dark Seducer]] equipment from ''[[SI:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]]''.
