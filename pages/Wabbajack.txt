==Notes==
*Wabbajack can also turn a creature into a [[Legends:Sweetroll|Sweetroll]].
*Wabbajack was formerly of the [[Legends:Intelligence|Intelligence]] attribute, but this was changed during the closed beta period.
*The [[Lore:Wabbajack|Wabbajack]] is the artifact of the [[Lore:Daedric Prince|Daedric Prince]] [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]].
*A physical copy of this card was given out at the Legends booth at [[wikipedia:Penny Arcade Expo|PAX]] East 2016. The physical copy is just a promotional item and doesn't provide access to the in-game card.
