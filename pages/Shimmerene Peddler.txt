==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Brynjolf's Heist|Brynjolf's Heist]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck as a potential reward from [[LG:Tangled Web|Tangled Web]].

==Notes==
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset]] city of [[Lore:Shimmerene|Shimmerene]].
*One of the summoning lines is a reference to [[Skyrim:Fihada|Fihada]] who says "will you also be needing the bow that shoots rainbows?" after you asked for arrows that can kill dragons.
