In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], three copies are included in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.
