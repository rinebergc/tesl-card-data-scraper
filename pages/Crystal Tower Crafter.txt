==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck.

== Notes ==
*This card's ability applies the '''Secrets Unlocked''' card effect.
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]].
*Prior to January 2017, this card was of 2/2 power and health. It was changed due to its tendency to gain too much power and health.{{ref|name=Jan2017|[https://legends.bethesda.net/articles/letter-from-the-devs-jan-2017 Letter from the Devs] from Bethesda.net, January 11 2017}}
