==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Imperial Might (deck)|Imperial Might]] deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.
