==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Night Mother's Crypt Part 2|Night Mother's Crypt Part 2]]. The premium version can currently only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Night Mother's Crypt Part 2.

==Notes==
*The premium version was originally available as part of [[LG:Purchases#The_Fall_of_the_Dark_Brotherhood_Prepurchase|The Fall of the Dark Brotherhood Prepurchase]] promotion.
*The [[Lore:Night Mother|Night Mother]] is an important figure within the Dark Brotherhood. The depiction in the card's art is how she appeared in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]].
*The card's art was created by [[:Category:Legends-Art-Nuare Studio|Nuare Studio]].
