==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] theme deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Alduin's Apocalypse|Alduin's Apocalypse]] theme deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Hlaalu Schemes|Hlaalu Schemes]] theme deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Tribunal Glory|Tribunal Glory]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card was previously part of the [[Legends:Training|training]] mechanic where it could be upgraded from {{Card|Hive Worker}} at level 4.
*An alternate-art version of this card was available in the [[LG:Mad Prince Pack|Mad Prince Pack]].
*[[Morrowind:Kwama|Kwama]] first appeared in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'', and later [[Online:Kwama|reappeared]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]. For more information about Kwama, see the [[Lore:Kwama|lore article]].
