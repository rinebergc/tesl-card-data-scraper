==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Thornwell Farm|Thornwell Farm]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Thornwell Farm.

==Notes==
*This card's Treasure Hunt applies the '''Strike It Rich''' card effect.
*The name of the card references the sewers of [[Lore:Riften|Riften]], which are referred to as the [[SR:The Ratway|Ratway]] and serve as a haven for the [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]] branch of the [[Lore:Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]] as well as the poor and assorted ne'er-do-wells of Riften.
