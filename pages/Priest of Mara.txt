==Availability==
Priest of Mara was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for May 2020. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards after February 2019, the premium version was awarded to the top 1000 [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]] players or can be obtained through [[LG:Soul Summoning|soul summoning]].
