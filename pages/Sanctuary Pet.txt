==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Falkreath Sanctuary|Falkreath Sanctuary]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Falkreath Sanctuary.

==Notes==
*This card is based on [[Skyrim:Lis|Lis]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'', who is referred to as the Dark Brotherhood's pet spider in the [[Skyrim:Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary|Falkreath Sanctuary]].
