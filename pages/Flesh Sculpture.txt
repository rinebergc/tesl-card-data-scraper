==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Princess Barynia|Princess Barynia]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Princess Barynia.

==Notes==
*The card's art depicts [[Skyrim:Galathil|Galathil]], a [[Lore:Flesh Sculpture|flesh sculptor]] who appeared in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''.
*If you activate Flesh Sculpture, it will change your in-game avatar until the end of the match.
