==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], three copies are included in the [[Legends:Ancano's Cunning|Ancano's Cunning]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Brynjolf's Heist|Brynjolf's Heist]] theme deck, three copies in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck, and two copies in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The [[Skyrim:Firebolt|Firebolt]] spell first appeared in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''.
*The card was earlier known as "Lesser Fireball" in development, and the earlier art was created by Nuare Studio. It was later reused as an alternate art reward for the Grand Melee on January 2020.
