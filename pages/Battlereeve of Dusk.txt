==Availability==
Battlereeve of Dusk was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for November 2016. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*This card's summon ability applies the '''Dusky Skill''' card effect.
*Note that only [[Legends:Keywords|Keywords]] will work, and not other card effects.
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset]] city of [[Lore:Dusk|Dusk]].
