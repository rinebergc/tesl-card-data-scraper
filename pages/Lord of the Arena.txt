==Notes==
*This card is a reference to the [[Oblivion:Arena_Combatants#Grand_Champion_Matches|Grand Champion matches]] you can take part in as a member of the [[Oblivion:Arena (faction)|Imperial City Arena faction]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion]]''. At higher levels you can face [[OB:Minotaur|Minotaur]]s and [[OB:Minotaur Lord|Minotaur Lord]]s in the Arena. This card also depicts a Minotaur wielding a [[OB:Dwarven Warhammer|Dwarven Warhammer]], similar to Minotaurs in ''Oblivion'' once your character reaches level 6.
