==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, three copies are included in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Ashlanders|Ashlanders]] of [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]], and the card's audio alludes to the Ashlander-[[Lore:Tribunal Temple|Tribunal Temple]] schism.
*The '[[Lore:Veloth|Veloth]]' mentioned in the card's attack audio was a [[Lore:Dark Elf|Dark Elf]] saint who led the [[Lore:Chimer|Chimer]] people to the land of [[Lore:Resdayn|Resdayn]].
*The ashlander depicted in the card's art is clad in the garb of a [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]] ashlander as it [[Online:Ashlander Style|appeared]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]].
