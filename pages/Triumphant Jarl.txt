==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Aela's Companions|Aela's Companions]] theme deck, two copies in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck, one copy in the [[Legends:Band of Survivors|Band of Survivors]] deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck.
