==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck.

Premium alternate art versions of this card were available as a reward from the July 2021 [[LG:Retro Singleton Gauntlet|Retro Singleton Gauntlet]].

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Swift''' card effect.
*The alternate art depicts the [[Lore:Gatekeeper|Gatekeeper]], a [[Legends:Atronach|flesh atronach]] that first [[SI:Gatekeeper|appeared]] in the ''[[Shivering:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]]'' expansion for ''[[OB:Oblivion|TES IV: Oblivion]]''.
