==Availability==
Protector of the Mane was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for May 2017. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Warrior Guard''' card effect.
*The card is named for the [[Lore:Mane|Mane]], the spiritual leader of the [[Lore:Khajiit|Khajiit]]. It portrays one of the [[Lore:Warrior Guard|Warrior Guards]].
