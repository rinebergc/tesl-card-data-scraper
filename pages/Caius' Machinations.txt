==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Houses of Morrowind|Houses of Morrowind]] expansion, two copies are included in the [[Legends:Telvanni Ambition|Telvanni Ambition]] theme deck.

==Notes==
*This card applies the '''Machination''' card effect.
*There's a creature card with similar effects depicting Cosades, named [[Legends:Caius Cosades|Caius Cosades]].
*This card depicts [[Legends:Caius Cosades|Caius Cosades]], who also [[Morrowind:Caius Cosades|appeared]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''.
