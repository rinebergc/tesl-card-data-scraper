==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], two copies are included in the [[Legends:Warriors of Hammerfell|Warriors of Hammerfell]] deck, and one copy in the [[Legends:Redoran Onslaught|Redoran Onslaught]] theme deck.
