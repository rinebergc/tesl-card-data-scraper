==Availability==
Altmer Dragonknight was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for October 2018. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.

==Notes==
*The card is named for [[Online:Dragonknight|Dragonknight]]s, a class of fire-wielding NPCs and players in [[Online:Online|ESO]].
*The card's ability applies the '''Burning Heart''' card effect.
