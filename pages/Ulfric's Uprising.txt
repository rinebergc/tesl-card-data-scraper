==Availability==
It is available as part of the [[Legends:Forgotten Hero Collection|Forgotten Hero Collection]].

==Notes==
*If a creature is returned to your hand by another creature during Ulfric's Uprising sequence (such as with {{Card|Forsworn Guide}}) before it could use its own summon ability, the ability will still be activated from your hand.
*The card's name refers to the [[Lore:Wars#The Stormcloak Rebellion|Stormcloak Uprising]], which was spearheaded by [[Lore:Ulfric Stormcloak|Ulfric Stormcloak]]. Both of these appeared in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''.
*The card's magicka cost was increased from 6 to 7 in [[Legends:Patch/1.72.4|patch 1.72.4]].
