A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Mistveil Dungeon|Mistveil Dungeon]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Mistveil Dungeon.
