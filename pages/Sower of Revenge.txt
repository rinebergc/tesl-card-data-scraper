==Availability==
Sower of Revenge was the [[Legends:Monthly Card|monthly card]] for March 2017. It can be soul-summoned by anybody regardless of participation in [[Legends:Ranked|ranked]]. As with all other monthly cards, the premium version was not awarded to anybody and must be obtained through soul-summoning.
