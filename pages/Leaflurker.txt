==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is included in the [[Legends:Paarthurnax's Roar|Paarthurnax's Roar]] theme deck. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck as a reward from [[LG:A Knife in the Dark|A Knife in the Dark]]. One copy is also obtainable in the [[Legends:Dunmer Avengers|Dunmer Avengers]] deck as a reward from [[LG:A Knife in the Dark|A Knife in the Dark]].
