==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Collapsing Tunnel (quest)|Collapsing Tunnel]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Collapsing Tunnel.

==Notes==
*This card's summon ability applies the '''Begrimed''' card effect to the silenced creature.
*Verminous Fabricants first [[Tribunal:Verminous Fabricant|appeared]] in ''[[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''. They have also [[Online:Verminous Fabricant|appeared]] in ''[[Online:Online|The Elder Scrolls Online]].''
