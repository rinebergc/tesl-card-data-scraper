==Availability==
A playset of the card is awarded for completing [[Legends:Shaft of Sohleh|Shaft of Sohleh]]. The premium version can only be obtained by [[Legends:Soul Gems#Soul Summoning|soul-summoning]], which is only possible after the completion of Shaft of Sohleh.

==Notes==
*The card is based on the Hunter-Killers [[Online:Hunter-Killer Positrox|Positrox]] and [[Online:Hunter-Killer Negatrix|Negatrix]] from [[ON:Online|ESO]].
