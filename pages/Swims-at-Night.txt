==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[LG:Heroes of Skyrim|Heroes of Skyrim]] expansion, an alternate art version is awarded for completing [[Legends:Chamber of Lorkhan|Chamber of Lorkhan]] on master mode.

==Notes==
*This card's ability applies the '''Politics of Contraband''' card effect.
*Despite this card's inclusion in the Heroes of Skyrim expansion, Swims-at-Night did not actually appear in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''. Rather, he is a character from [[Legends:The Forgotten Hero|The Forgotten Hero]] story.
