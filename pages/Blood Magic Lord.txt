==Blood Magic==
'''Blood Magic Spells''' are legendary Endurance cards, all with a cost of 2. When they are inserted into your hand, they have a red tint. Each of them is based on a power of [[Skyrim:Vampire Lord|Vampire Lord]]s in ''[[Skyrim:Dawnguard|Dawnguard]]''. A list of Blood Magic cards follows:

*{{Card|Corpse Curse}}
*{{Card|Drain Life}}
*{{Card|Gargoyle}}
*{{Card|Raise Dead}}

==Notes==
*This card was previously part of the [[Legends:Training|training]] mechanic where it could be upgraded from {{Card|Volkihar Lord}} at level 50.
*A physical copy of this card was given out at the Legends booth at [[wikipedia:Penny Arcade Expo|PAX]] East 2016. The physical copy is just a promotional item and doesn't provide access to the in-game card.
*Using the Blood Magic spells provided by this card contributes towards a [[LG:Titles|title]].
