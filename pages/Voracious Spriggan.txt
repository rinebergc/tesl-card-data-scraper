==Availability==
In addition to the card's availability in the [[Legends:Core Set|Core Set]], one copy is obtainable in the [[Legends:Black Marsh Smugglers|Black Marsh Smugglers]] deck as a reward from [[LG:Unwelcome Intrusion|Unwelcome Intrusion]].

==Notes==
*[[Daggerfall:Spriggan|Spriggan]]s first appeared in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]'', and have since been a fixture in the series, reappearing in ''[[Bloodmoon:Spriggan|Bloodmoon]]'', ''[[Oblivion:Spriggan|Oblivion]]'', ''[[Skyrim:Spriggan|Skyrim]]'', and [[Online:Spriggan|ESO]]. For more information on Spriggans, see the [[Lore:Spriggan|lore article]]. 
* This was originally a rare card, but was changed around 10/2016 to help balance quality of common cards.{{ref|name=LetterDevs|[https://legends.bethesda.net/articles/letter-from-the-devs Letter from the Devs] on Bethesda.net, September 29, 2016}}
