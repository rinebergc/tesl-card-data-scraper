==Notes==
*The card is based on [[Online:Veya Releth|Veya Releth]], who appeared in [[Online:Online|ESO]] as a [[Lore:Morag Tong|Morag Tong]] apprentice and later an agent of [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]].  It was also voiced by Veya's actress, Helen Sadler.
*The card's summon audio alludes to [[Legends:Naryu Virian|Naryu Virian]], who first [[Online:Naryu Virian|appeared]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]].
*The card text was changed to remove "Copy" from the textbox in [[Legends:Patch/2.12|Patch 2.12]].
