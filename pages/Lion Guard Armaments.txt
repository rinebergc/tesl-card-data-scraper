==Notes==
*The {{Card|Recruit}} created by this card is a [[Legends:Redguard|Redguard]].
*The name of the card refers to the [[Lore:Lion Guard|Lion Guard]], an elite group of warriors who served as the personal guard of [[Lore:Emeric|King Emeric]] of [[Lore:Wayrest|Wayrest]], the leader of the [[Lore:Daggerfall Covenant|Daggerfall Covenant]] during the [[Lore:Alliance War|Alliance War]]. The Lion Guard first appeared in [[Online:Online|ESO]]. 
*The armor itself depicts the [[Online:Daggerfall Covenant Style|armor style]] of the Daggerfall Covenant.
